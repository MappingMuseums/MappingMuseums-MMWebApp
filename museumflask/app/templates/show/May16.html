Museum events

1). Year museum opened to the public

2). Year museum closed to the public

3). Physical museum changes:

amalgamation: two museums close and one new museum opens 
merge: one museum joins (merges into) another museum
group: two (or more) museums are formed into a larger entity

4). Change of museum location (e.g. move from Doncaster to York)

5). Change of museum governance status (e.g. local authority to independent)
